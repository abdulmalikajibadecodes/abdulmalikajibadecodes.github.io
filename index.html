Intelligent Predictive Maintenance: Cost-Aware Classification of Critical Equipment Failures

In this project, I developed machine learning models for classifying equipment failure types to proactively identify specific failure modes before breakdowns occur. By implementing cost-sensitive learning techniques, the model prioritizes accuracy in high-cost failure predictions, effectively reducing downtime and maintenance expenses. This approach allows for early intervention on critical failures, enhancing overall equipment reliability and operational efficiency.

Energy Mix Analysis: Data-Driven Insights for Sustainable Energy Strategies

In this project, I analyzed Spain’s energy generation data (2015–2018) to identify key trends, challenges, and opportunities in the country’s energy transition. Through data-driven insights on nuclear, fossil fuels, and renewables, I uncovered patterns in seasonal usage, demand gaps, and the interplay between conventional and renewable sources.The findings provide actionable intelligence for optimizing energy strategies and driving sustainable, data-driven decision-making in the energy sector..

Well Production Optimization: Hybrid CRM-ML Framework for Enhanced Reservoir Management

This project demonstrates the innovative combination of traditional Capacitance-Resistance Models (CRM) with modern machine learning techniques to optimize oil well production rates. By developing a hybrid approach that leverages both physics-based modeling and advanced analytics, I achieved significant improvements in production forecasting accuracy, reducing prediction errors by up to 73% in challenging wells..

Engineered a comprehensive machine learning solution for oil production forecasting, leveraging multiple advanced models including LSTM networks and XGBoost. Developed and deployed a web application for real-time predictions, achieving 80% improvement over traditional forecasting methods.
